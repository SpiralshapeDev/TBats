Terraria Bats Mod
Discord [ https://discord.com/invite/A7wBSSFfAD ]

The Terraria Bats Mod adds a new weapon type for melee Terraria, the Bat! These Bats can be extremely useful in tight situations when you need to deal a lot of damage suddenly or hit an enemy away from you!


Here is a breakdown of all the content available in this mod currently:

23 new configs for each weapon!
4 new projectiles!
23 new recipes!
23 new weapons!
1 new menu screen!
